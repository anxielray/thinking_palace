About Thinking Palace

Welcome to Thinking Palace, a space designed not for the average thinker, but for those of us who constantly seek structure in chaos and elegance in equations.

My name is Raymond Martin, and I built this place as an open archive of mathematical and scientific deductions, abstract connections, and mental freedom. I'm an INTP — a thinker, a concept synthesizer, a rebel against the limits of shallow thought.

“In a world where intellect is commodified, I choose to give it away for free.”

Thinking Palace releases new content weekly — mathematical patterns, scientific thought, and interdisciplinary insights. If you've ever wanted to stretch your neurons without limits, you’re in the right place.

No paywalls. No fluff. Just raw thought.
